# Strategy Review Session

## Problem/Feature Description

A startup founder has written a go-to-market strategy for their new SaaS product. The strategy is at `inputs/strategy.md`. They want it stress-tested before they present it to investors next week.

They've asked you to grill them on the strategy — they know the plan has gaps and want them surfaced now, not in the investor meeting. They want you to be thorough and not stop at the obvious surface-level issues.

Produce a document capturing the full grilling of the strategy.

## Output Specification

Write the grilling session to `grill-session.md`. Number each question sequentially.
