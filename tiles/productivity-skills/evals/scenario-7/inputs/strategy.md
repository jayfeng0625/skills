# Go-to-Market Strategy: FleetTrack Pro

## What We're Building

FleetTrack Pro is a SaaS platform for small logistics companies (5–50 vehicles) to track their fleets in real time, optimize routes, and generate compliance reports. Target customers are regional delivery companies, HVAC contractors, and medical supply couriers.

## Target Launch

We plan to launch in 6 months. We have a working MVP with basic GPS tracking.

## Pricing

We're thinking $49/month per vehicle, billed monthly. No annual discount for now.

## Distribution

Our main channel will be direct sales — we'll hire two sales reps immediately after funding. We'll also attend two trade shows (logistics industry) in the first year.

## Key Differentiator

Unlike the big players (Verizon Connect, Samsara), we'll have a simpler UI and faster onboarding. Customers can be live in under an hour.

## Customer Acquisition

We expect word of mouth to drive 40% of growth after the first 6 months.

## Competitive Response

We haven't modeled what happens if competitors cut prices in response to our entry.

## Team

Founding team: two engineers, one designer. No sales or marketing hires yet.
