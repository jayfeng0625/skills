# Architecture Design Review

## Problem/Feature Description

An engineering team has written a design proposal for refactoring their notification system. The proposal is at `inputs/design-proposal.md`. There is also existing source code in `inputs/src/` that shows how notifications are currently implemented.

The team lead wants the design grilled before the proposal goes to the wider team for feedback. They want every assumption challenged, every decision examined, and every open question surfaced and answered.

Grill the design proposal. For each question you raise, provide your recommended answer.

## Output Specification

Write your output to `design-grill.md`. Structure it as numbered questions, each followed immediately by your recommendation.
